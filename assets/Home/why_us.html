BEYOND ACADEMICS

Infusing Confidence to face the world

Personality Development and Grooming

At F-TEC, we take due care of the Personality Development too. It is a focus area because a good percentage of success depends upon the confidence and personality that the students exhibit while facing the interview. An important part of course curriculum is enhancement of confidence in students, be it academic or personality. To help our students in grooming, special sessions are held on work readiness, business etiquettes and effective communication. The methodology followed is by presentation and discussion of techniques for gaining competitive edge, practice of public speaking and individual attention through mock sessions.

Our students have made us proud!

F-TEC strives hard to connect the prospective employers with the right channel ie our students all groomed to face the world. All our programs are so designed to inculcate the required computer skills, functional skills and soft skills in students and make them ready to face the workplace with minimum intervention. Our alumni have been placed in leading companies in the public sector, private sector and multinationals. We are also proud to mention a good number have become entrepreneurs too.

This achievement has been the result of the dedicated staff at F-TEC Centre, placement networking team that constantly interacts with the corporate world and helps prepare students for their careers. Job Awareness Guidance & Opportunity (JAGO) is planned to ensure complete awareness among students about the career prospects in the market so that they can choose the right career path. All successful candidates under Diploma Programs are supported through centralised placement assistance to make sure that they get the right kind of employment.

Job Connect Programs are organized every month. The placement drives, enabled by the F-TEC Placement Team, wherein one or more corporate entities from various sectors across the industry are invited to interview and hire skilled candidates by following their hiring process. Through this mutually beneficial arrangement, corporates are able to recruit certified, skilled employees in bulk and at a minimal cost.
